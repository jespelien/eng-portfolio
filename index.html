01 — About

I currently work on analog and mixed-signal circuit design, embedded systems, and system integration of complex electrical systems.

I am interested in deep-tech startups and venture capital.

Various projects I've worked on include the Apple Watch, projectile guidance systems for the Navy, Columbia FSAE's Battery Management System, an EV Mazda Miata conversion, a hobbyist fusion reactor, a low-cost brain–computer interface, a standalone GPS-speedometer for marine and automotive use, and an investment thesis on distributed compute for a VC firm.
